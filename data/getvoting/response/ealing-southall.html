Why vote tactically? keyboard_arrow_down

Tactical voting works

Our recommendations are data-led, but we also take into account the local political situation. We used MRP analysis of polls conducted in October 2019 with a sample size of 46,000 voters. Another MRP conducted in November 2019 with a sample of over 39,000. And a final MRP in early December from a sample of 28,272. Our final recommendations take account of the current situation and the trends over all three MRPs.
