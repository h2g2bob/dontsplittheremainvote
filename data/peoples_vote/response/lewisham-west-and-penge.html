Fight for a People's Vote

This is the fight of our lifetime, and we need all hands on deck where it matters most. That’s why we’re focusing our efforts on battleground constituencies that will not only determine who is elected in these areas, but also whether we win the fight for a People’s Vote.

We need you (and a few of your friends!) to get involved and help us win the fight for a People’s Vote. Check below to see where your nearest battleground constituencies are, and the campaigning events you can attend. Click on an event to sign up and help us get a People’s Vote!
